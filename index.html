Privacy Policy — Gawadzilla POS

Last updated: 15 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gawadzilla POS ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the app you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who we are

Gawadzilla POS is a point-of-sale application for small businesses. For any privacy questions, contact us at gawadzilla.lb@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Google Account information — when you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. This is used to identify your account and secure your data.
  • Business data you create — products, categories, sales, receipts, customer credit ("pay later") accounts, staff accounts, and business settings that you enter into the app.
  • Backup data — if you use the optional Google Drive backup feature, a copy of your business data is saved to a folder in your own Google Drive.

We do not collect your device location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide core app functionality (recording sales, managing inventory, generating receipts and reports).
  • To authenticate you and keep your data in sync across your devices.
  • To let you back up and restore your data to your own Google account.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud service provided by Google, under your authenticated account. Optional backups are stored in your own Google Drive. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections.

5. Third-party services

We rely on the following Google services, each governed by Google's own privacy policy:

  • Google Sign-In (authentication)
  • Google Firebase Authentication & Firestore (secure data storage and sync)
  • Google Drive API (optional backup, drive.file scope — access limited to files the app creates)

See Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles data.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your data at any time by removing individual records within the app, deleting your Google Drive backup file, or by contacting us at gawadzilla.lb@gmail.com to request full deletion of your account data. We will action verified deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Children's privacy

Gawadzilla POS is a business tool intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Security

We use Google's authentication and security infrastructure to protect your data, including encryption in transit and access controls that restrict your data to your own authenticated account.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact: gawadzilla.lb@gmail.com.
